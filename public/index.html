Good to Know

Is this real career counseling?

No. This tool provides data-driven suggestions based on federal labor databases. It does not replace professional career counseling.

Where does the data come from?

All occupation data comes from O*NET, salary data from the Bureau of Labor Statistics, and federal job listings from USAJOBS.

Is my conversation saved?

Conversations are stored temporarily during your session. No permanent account is created and no data is retained after you leave.

What are the limitations?

Data may lag behind real-time market changes. The AI can make mistakes. Skills matching uses standardized O*NET taxonomies which may not cover every niche role.

ℹ️

Important Note

AI-generated career advice is informational only and does not constitute professional counseling. Always consult qualified career professionals for major career decisions. Data sourced from O*NET, BLS, and USAJOBS may not reflect the very latest labor market changes.
