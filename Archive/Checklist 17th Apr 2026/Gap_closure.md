## Implementation sequence (recommended)

1. Execute **P1** first (stabilizes failure behavior and user trust).
2. Execute **P2** next (prevents reintroduction of known issues).
3. Execute **P3** in parallel with late P2 (locks deterministic orchestration behavior).
4. Execute **P4** after P1/P3 controls are in place (session continuity becomes measurable and robust).

## Governance for closure

- Update these files after each priority completes: `architecture_target_vs_current.md`, `project_plan_comp_checklist.md`, `Checklist_claude_summary.md`.
- Move status from **Partial** to **Yes/Strong** only when exit criteria and CI evidence are both satisfied.
